Habitué Description

Habitué application is a type of software that helps you create, track, and maintain good habits. The app allows you to set goals for yourself, such as exercising for 30 minutes a day, meditating for 10 minutes a day, or reading for 15 minutes a day, and then track your progress over time.

When you complete a habit, you can log it in the app, and the app will keep track of your progress. This can help you see how consistent you are with your habits and help you stay motivated to keep going.

Many habit tracker apps also include features like reminders, habit streaks, and rewards to help you stay on track and make the habit-building process more fun and engaging. Some apps also provide data and analytics to help you see your progress over time and make adjustments to your goals or habits as needed.

Overall, a habit tracker app is a useful tool for anyone looking to build or maintain good habits, whether it's exercising regularly, eating healthier, meditating, or practicing any other habit you want to develop.

Project Goals

Habitué App main goal is to make it easy for users to create and track their habits.
The app should have reminders, progress tracking, and insights to help users stay motivated and make adjustments to their habits.
The app needs to be reliable, secure, and work on different devices to reach a wider audience, with the ultimate goal of helping users build and maintain good habits
